MISSION VALUES VISION

To breed, raise, and produce superior cattle for the commercial meat market through a vertically coordinated alliance  and thus provide meat lovers of all ages with fresh, high quality produce that is reasonably priced, expertly prepared and served with care, all the while striving to provide the best customer service to our customers before and after the sale by taking an active interest in their needs, understanding them, and tailoring our meat products to respond to their requirements at highly competitive pricing levels; our measures of success for doing so effectively are customer acceptance, personal satisfaction, and profit.

To be humane towards all animals and to treat them with the necessary respect by creating allow stressed farm - feedlot and abattoir environment by maintaining animal friendly facilities and using modern abattoir technology and slaughter , approved welfare societies.

  • QUALITY SERVICE

  • QUALITY PRODUCTS
  • ENSURING FOOD SAFETY
  • INTEGRITY
  • VIABILITY OF THE INDUSTRY

We aspire to be the undisputed market leader in the supply of quality meat and meat products in Zimbabwe by adhering to the above mission statement, and being governed by the principles and values that follow in so doing, make Bellevue well known in household brand associated with quality and freshness.
